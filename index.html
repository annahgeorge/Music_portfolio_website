About

A little about Annah

Annah George is a composer and musician from Houston, Texas, studying at the Royal Northern College of Music under Dr. Anna Appleby and Dr. Larry Goves.

Her artistic voice is shaped by a foundation in classical violin and pop vocal performance, resulting in a style that moves fluidly between genres and disciplines. At the core of her music is a deep commitment to melody and lyricism, with a natural instinct to treat every instrument as if it were singing its own line.

❦

She explores themes of love, beauty, humanity, and playfulness, often balancing rhythmic vitality with music that carries depth and invites the listener into deeper reflection.

Annah aims to pursue a career as a film composer, concert composer, and performing recording artist.
